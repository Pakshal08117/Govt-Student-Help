• पतीचे मृत्यू प्रमाणपत्र

• बँक खाते

❓ तुम्हाला चांगल्या प्रकारे मदत करण्यासाठी:
• तुमची किती मुले आहेत?
• त्यांचे वय काय आहे?
• ते शाळेत जातात का?

## 🚀 How to Use

### Step 1: Open AI Assistant
Click the orange pulsing button (bottom-right)

### Step 2: Click Microphone 🎤
Wait for "🎤 Speak now..."

### Step 3: Speak Your Situation
Tell your real problem in your own words:
- "My husband died, I have kids"
- "I lost my job"
- "I am pregnant"
- "My child is disabled"

### Step 4: Listen to AI Response
- AI understands your situation
- Provides empathetic response
- Suggests relevant schemes
- Offers to help apply
- Asks follow-up questions

### Step 5: Continue Conversation
Answer AI's questions or ask more:
- "Yes, help me apply"
- "Tell me more"
- "What documents do I need?"
